Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to the "Hexagon Highway" app for mobile devices, together with any related services operated by Mirak Studio (collectively, the "Application"). Mirak Studio is hereby referred to as the "Service Provider".

Information Collection and Use

The Application collects information when you download and use it. This information may include:

  • Your device's Internet Protocol (IP) address (Approximate Location)
  • Device and Advertising IDs (such as Android Advertising ID or Apple IDFA)
  • User IDs and Nicknames provided by authentication platforms
  • App activity data, including gameplay interactions, crash reports, and performance diagnostics
  • The mobile operating system you use
Authentication and Cloud Save

To enable cross-device progress and cloud saving, the Application offers optional sign-in through third-party services like Google Play Games Services and Apple Game Center. If you choose to log in, we collect your unique User ID and gamer nickname provided by these platforms. This data is strictly used for account management and app functionality (saving your progress).

Advertising and Analytics

The Application utilizes third-party advertising networks and mediation platforms (Google AdMob, ironSource LevelPlay, Unity Ads, AppLovin) and analytics tools (Unity Analytics) to keep the game free to play and improve its performance. These third parties may collect and share your Advertising ID, IP address, and app interaction data to deliver personalized (or non-personalized) advertisements and analyze game usage.

For Users in the EU/UK: We utilize a Consent Management Platform (Google UMP SDK) to ask for your permission before collecting data for personalized ads, ensuring compliance with the GDPR and ePrivacy Directive. If you decline, you will receive non-personalized ads instead.

Account and Data Deletion

You have the right to request the immediate and permanent deletion of your account, cloud save, and associated data from our servers. You can easily delete your data directly within the game via the Settings menu, or you can request manual deletion via email. Detailed instructions on both methods can be found on our dedicated Account Deletion Page.

Voluntary User Feedback

If you choose to submit feedback or bug reports through our external feedback forms, we use a third-party service provider called Tally (Tally B.V.). When you access the form, Tally processes your IP address and any information you voluntarily enter. This data is collected solely for the purpose of evaluating game performance and resolving bugs.

Your Rights & California Privacy Rights (CCPA/CPRA)

Depending on your region, you may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data held by the Service Provider. If you are a California resident, you have the right to know what personal information is collected, the right to delete personal information, the right to opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information, and the right to non-discrimination for exercising these rights. To exercise your rights, contact the Service Provider at mobile@sehilistudio.com.

Data Retention Policy

The Service Provider retains personal data based on its necessity for the stated purposes: Cloud save data and User IDs are stored as long as your account is active and are deleted immediately upon account deletion request. Automatically collected technical data is retained for up to 24 months from collection. Aggregated and anonymized data is retained indefinitely.

Children

The Application is designed for users aged 13 and older. The Service Provider does not knowingly collect or solicit personal data from children under 13 years of age. If the Service Provider discovers that a child under 13 has provided personal information, this data will be immediately deleted from the servers.
