The Service Provider retains personal data based on its necessity for the stated purposes: Automatically collected technical data (like crash reports or basic system metrics) is retained for up to 24 months from collection, unless longer retention is required for legal compliance. The Application is designed for users aged 13 and older. The Service Provider does not knowingly collect or solicit personal data from children under 13 years of age.
